Prices

All prices include:

  • Private transfers from and to the airport
  • Accommodation in a double or single bedroom, with air conditioning
  • Breakfast and lunch (local cuisine)
  • Complementary coffee and tea
  • Daily shuttle service to Kota Kinabalu town

The price does not include flights or insurance, or optional meals (lunch and dinner)

Shaolin Kung Fu / Qi Gong Training

  • £650 per week/per person

Retreat Only

  • £48 per day/single room; £68 per day/double room

How to Book

Please send us your booking enquiry using our online booking form. We will email you with details of availability for your chosen dates. Once you are ready to confirm, we will require a 25% deposit to reserve your holiday. If you have any questions you would like to raise with us before placing your booking, please use our contact form.

Payment

We accept the following methods of payment:

  • PayPal – We will send you a PayPal invoice by email if you wish to use this method
  • Bank Transfer – We will provide you the bank details to make the transfer

Booking Conditions

  • Minimum stay of 1 week
  • Booking will be confirmed only after a 25% deposit has been received
  • The full fee must be paid two months before your arrival date
  • If the full fees are not paid two months before arrival, the proprietors reserve the right to cancel the booking

Cancellation charges

  • 4 weeks prior to arrival date: 20% of full fee
  • 3 weeks prior to arrival date: 30% of full fee
  • 2 weeks prior to arrival date: 60% of full fee
  • Less then 1 week prior to arrival date: 80% of full fee

Check-in/Check-out

  • Check-in time: 1.00pm
  • Check-out time: 12.00pm
